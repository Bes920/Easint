Platform overview

What users can investigate inside Easint.

Files

Hash lookup, upload analysis, and metadata extraction for suspicious documents and media.

Infrastructure

IP reputation, geolocation, reverse IP, DNS, WHOIS, SSL, and subdomain enumeration.

People and identities

Email OSINT, breach checks, username hunting, and related public account discovery.

Learning and OPSEC

Training-oriented content for safer investigations, metadata awareness, and privacy hygiene.

Built for authorized research, education, and legitimate investigations.
